Simple Term Study

Welcome, new user!

This is a very simple application for creating multiple choice quizzes for studying a list of terms.

Important Notes:

  • All of your progress will be stored locally in your browser.
  • If you move to a different browser, you will not have access to this progress.
  • If you reset your broswer or clear your cache, the progress will be deleted.
  • You have the option (under user settings) to save your progress to a file, which you can load later or elsewhere.
  • NONE of your information will be tracked anywhere outside of your own browser.

If you acknowledge the above, please set your name so that you can track your progress.

If you are looking for a more robust learning tool, consider PidginForge.org, which is a labor of love and perpetual work in progress.
